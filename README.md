# Business Problem Statement

## Decision to be Made

Determine whether the new onboarding and activation campaign (Treatment Group) should be launched to all users based on the results of the A/B experiment.

## Who the Decision Impacts

* New users signing up for the product
* Product Management team
* Marketing team
* Customer Success team
* Business leadership

## Metric That Should Improve

The primary objective is to increase the percentage of users who convert from trial to paid subscription while also improving onboarding completion and early user engagement.

## Risks to Monitor

* Increase in customer support tickets
* Higher refund requests
* Lower user engagement
* Longer time to convert
* Negative impact on customer experience

## Evidence Required Before Making a Recommendation

Before recommending a full rollout, compare the Control and Treatment groups using key business metrics such as:

* Trial start rate
* Onboarding completion rate
* Paid conversion rate
* Revenue generated within 30 days
* Engagement score
* Support tickets
* Refund requests

The recommendation should only be made if the Treatment group demonstrates improved business performance without causing significant negative effects on customer experience or operational metrics.
